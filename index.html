About

Emerging from Toronto's vibrant electronic music scene, Julian Ross is a DJ dedicated to exploring the depths of Afro house, deep house, and melodic techno.

His musical journey is guided by a philosophy of reaching into the soul's deepest chambers, creating sonic experiences that aim to whistle at the void.
